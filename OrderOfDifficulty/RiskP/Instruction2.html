In this part,

[Your tasks] You will have to make 11 decisions in the specific senario shown below:

Decision Box

Imagine there is an opaque box containing 5 blue balls and 5 red balls.

At each decision point, you are required to choose between two options:

  • Option A: Draw a ball from the box. If the drawn ball is blue, you receive 10 points
  • Option B: Directly receive X points without drawing a ball

where the value of X differs at each decision point.

There is no right or wrong choice, please make decisions based on your preferences.

[Your earnings] In case you are eligible for the bonus, at the end of the experiment, one of the 11 decision points will be randomly selected by computer. If you select option A in the chosen decision point, then the computer will randomly draw a ball from the box; if it is a blue ball, you will get 10 points, if not, you will get 0 points. If you select option B in the chosen decision point, then you will directly get the corresponding X points.
