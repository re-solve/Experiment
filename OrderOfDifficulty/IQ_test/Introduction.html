You will now take part in an economic experiment. This experiment will take approximately 20 minutes. You will receive a show-up fee of $3 for participating. The show-up fee will be paid at the end of the experiment.

The experiment consists of two parts and a short survey. Detailed instructions will be shown at the beginning of each part.

There is a 20% chance that you will win additional bonus up $15 (total up to $18) . In case you are eligible for the bonus, how much extra bonus you can earn depends on your decisions in both Part 1 and Part 2. Detailed payment rule will be shown at the beginning of each part. In this experiment, we talk about "points", and all the points you earned will be converted into $ at a certain rate and paid along with the show-up fees at the end of the experiment .
